Chicken Fried Rice

Chicken Fried Rice - Serves 3

Ingredients

  • 2 teaspoons paprika
  • 3/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon Cayenne pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon origano
  • 1/2 teaspoon thyme
  • 320g basmati rice
  • 320ml water
  • 2 chicken breasts
  • 2 eggs
  • Broccoli, peas and carrots to taste
  • Splash of soy sauce
  • Dash of olive oil

Steps

  1. In a small pot mix together the spices/herbs (all ingredients above the rice)
  2. Crack two eggs into a small bowl, mix yolk and whites
  3. Pat down chicken breast with a paper towel and dice
  4. Pour over then rub in spice mixture
  5. Set the chicken to one side
  6. Wash rice, then add to pressure cooker along with water and a dash of soy sauce. Stir to distribute the soy sauce
  7. Within pressure cooker, place mini bowl and stand into the rice then fill with vegetables as desired
  8. Set pressure cooker to cook on medium pressure for 12 minutes
  9. Preheat pan on medium heat
  10. Once pressure cooker has 10 minutes remaining add chicken to pan
  11. Once rice is cooked, remove from pressure cooker, place veg on the side and add rice to pan.
  12. Increase temperature to high, make space in the centre of the pan, add egg. Use spatula to break and spread egg before mixing into main rice body
  13. Serve with vegetables

Description

It sure isn't perfect, but its what I've come to like as a comfort food.
