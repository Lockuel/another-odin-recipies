Chicken Fried Rice

An apple - Serves 1

Ingredients

  • An apple of some kind

Steps

  1. Collect knife from cupboard
  2. Slice apple into quaters
  3. Carefully (with the knife facing away from your hands and body) cut out the core from each slice
  4. Enjoy
